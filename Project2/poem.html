Pahli baar jab tumhara didar kiya tha taab se hi ho gaya tha pyar

to tamanna kuch aur thi magar ishwar ki ichha aur thi yaar.

Tumhari haar baat har gun-gunahat gunje mere kano mei

Ishq Mera sachha tha itna pyar Ram bhi Siya ko na kar paye saat janmo mei.

Hamara milna ittefaq tha to mai dua Karu upar wale sei

Aise Dil kaise lagaye do ajnabio k mulakato se ?

Tum aate aate Mai khudko kaha kho diya

Tumhari khusi ko apna manke Ishq ko mukammal kar liya.

Mera pyar kafi hai na itna mujhe bata do

Tumhare haar gaam k aansu mere haatho se mita lo.

Din mei ek jhalak dekhne k liye tarse mera pagal maan

Tumare liye mera pyar kaise barhta rahe har ek shaan.
